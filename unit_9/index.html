Paradox of Fools

Agent of Chaos

A novel about a bridge and other topics by Clare Coppel

Paradox of Fools explores the ways in which ideas are shared via the internet. Paradox of Fools is dedicated to Kurt Vonnegut and butterflies. It's kind of a shitty novel that humbly seeks to disrupt the selfies and the memes of social media. Paradox of Fools can be read in either direction on Instagram.
